Shoulder & Back

Shoulder exercises help keep the shoulder joint and surrounding muscles limber, thereby increasing shoulder function.

Back exercises strengthens the muscles that support the spine, removing pressure from the spinal discs and facet joints. It also alleviates stiffness and improves mobility.
